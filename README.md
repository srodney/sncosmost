sncosmost
=========

Extensions to Kyle Barbary's sncosmo package, principally for current and future space telescopes (HST,JWST,WFIRST).


* Defines HST band passes for ACS-WFC, WFC3-IR, and WFC3-UVIS.

* Adds new supernova models with extrapolated SEDs of SNANA CC SN templates 

* includes Pop III CC SN models from D. Whalen et al. 2013
